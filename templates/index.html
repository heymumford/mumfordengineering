About

I'm Eric Mumford — a senior architect who builds across the full spectrum of engineering. Whether it's fabricating custom aluminum heater boxes for classic Land Rover Defenders or designing multi-organ cognitive software systems, I apply the same principles: measure twice, build to last, test everything.

My background spans enterprise automation, knowledge graph systems, and hands-on fabrication. I believe the best engineering happens at the intersection of physical craft and computational thinking.

When I'm not writing code or bending metal, I write about philosophy, artificial intelligence, and the human side of technology.
